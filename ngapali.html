Ngapali

Ngapali Beach can be found in Rakhine, in Myanmar. It is the most well known beach of the country, visited by tourists from all over the world every year. Ngapali beach is situated on the Indian Ocean and is three kilometres long.Thandwe Airport provides tourists the opportunity to fly into Ngapali Beach.

Beach View

It is famous for its natural and unspotted beauty up to this day. The beach stretches about 3 km with soft white sand fringed by coconut palms. Ngapali Beach is one of the loveliest places in Myanmar.

Sunset View

Sunset, also known as sundown, is the daily disappearance of the Sun below the horizon due to Earth's rotation. ... As viewed from the middle latitudes, the local summer Sun sets to the northwest for the Northern Hemisphere, but to the southwest for the Southern Hemisphere.

Snorkeling

Snorkeling is the practice of swimming on or through a body of water while equipped with a diving mask, a shaped breathing tube called a snorkel, and usually swimfins. Snorkeling is a popular recreational activity, particularly at tropical resort locations.
